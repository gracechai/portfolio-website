My Story

After graduating Rutgers University in 2018 with a Doctorate in Physical Therapy, I discovered UX design and realized the possibility of combining my passion for helping people wiht my love for design and technology.

My background in healthcare has taught me to empathize with people and the importance of human-centered design. New challenges excite me and I love solving problems, so I am always looking for new ways to solves and iterate on existing problems. As a user experience designer, it is my mission to bridge the gap between the needs of people and possible technologies, and creating designs that are intuitive and useful.
